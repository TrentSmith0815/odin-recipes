"The Mix"

Description

You need something to gulp down during a delicious meal, try "The Mix"! (name pending)

Ingredients

  • 8-32 oz vessel for drinking
  • Dr. Pepper
  • A&W Root Beer
  • Optional: Ice

Steps

  1. Add your ice to your drinking vessel if desired
  2. Fill vessel with 40% Root Beer and 60% Dr. Pepper
    3. Note: you can play with the percentages, but this is my favorite>
  3. Enjoy with good friends
